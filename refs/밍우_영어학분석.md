Answer
- 용어정리
Constituent : strings of one or more words that syntactically and semantically behave as a unit. (멘토/신알규)
Movement Test : If we move a particular string of words in a sentence from one position to another,
then it behaves as a constituent. (멘토/신알규)
- 관련 이론 정리
Topicalisation(movements to the left = preposing)의 목적 : contrast (information processing), emphasizing
VP-preposing : modal auxiliary verbs are not part of the VP
The AdvP must be moved along with the main verb and its Direct Object, and is therefore inside
VP. Leaving the AdvP behind leads to an ungrammatical result.
- 지식의 틀
Movement test 중 topicalisation과 일부 VP-preposing을 통한 constituency 판단 문제이므로, 응용되어 나온다면 다른
movement test인 Heavy-NP-Shift(movements to the right), Extraposition of Subject Clauses, Extraposition
from NP로 밑줄 친 문장의 constituency 여부를 판단하는 문제가 나올 수 있을 것이다 (movement test 전체적으로 알아두기)
